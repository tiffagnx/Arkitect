# DeMartinville — Changelog

## v1.4.0 — The Beat Lab update

The big one: a whole new room for **making beats**, plus your own AI characters.

### 🥁 NEW ROOM — DeMartin Beat Lab
A pro, FL-Studio-style beat maker, built right in. No samples or downloads needed — it plays the moment you open it.
- **Floating-window workspace** — the Channel Rack, Mixer, Piano Roll, Playlist, and Instrument Browser are each their own window. Drag them anywhere, resize from any edge or corner, maximize, minimize, close, and click one to bring it to the front — just like a real DAW. Full menu bar (File · Edit · Add · Patterns · View · Options · Tools · Help).
- **Channel Rack step sequencer** — click steps to build a beat, per-step velocity, swing, multiple patterns.
- **An Instrument Browser with 80+ instruments** — guitars (real string modeling), pianos, electric pianos, organs, brass, strings, bells, mallets, synth leads, pads, plucks, basses, 808s, and a full drum kit. Search it, click to add.
- **Preset sound banks** on every instrument — pick "Trap Boom," "Clean 808," "Supersaw," "Church Organ," and dozens more.
- **An AI brain in every plugin** — open any instrument or effect, tap 🧠, and just talk to it ("make this 808 hit harder," "darker keys," "more slide"). It answers like a producer and turns its own knobs for you — safely.
- **Piano Roll** with scale highlighting + snap-to-scale, **Mixer** with faders and insert FX (EQ, Compressor, Reverb, Delay, Drive, Filter, Chorus, a one-knob "Glue," and a Limiter), **Export to .wav**, and **Save/Load**.
- **Drag in your own audio** to load it as a playable sampler.
- Kit knows his way around the room — ask him anything.

### 🧬 Characters
Build your own custom AI characters and drop them into any room to be your brain there.

### 📣 In-room Feedback
A Feedback button in every room — talk or type a bug or idea and it bundles it up clean.

### ✨ Polish
- DeMartinville branding across the rooms.
- Various fixes and refinements throughout.

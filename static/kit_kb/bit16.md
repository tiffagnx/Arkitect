# Bit1Six (bit16) — playable 16-bit game from a song

Turn a song into a playable 16-bit side-scroller and capture a run as a music
video. Deep how-to below.

## What Bit1Six is
A room that turns your song into a real, playable 16-bit side-scroller game — and
lets you record a playthrough as a music video. The level is built from your song.

## PLAY mode
PLAY mode is a real side-scroller: run, sprint, and jump through a level that's
generated from your track. Play it like a game — the action lines up with the
music.

## Recording a music video (REC)
Hit REC in-game to capture your run as a video. Play through the level to the
song, and the recorded run becomes your music video.

## DIRECTOR mode
The DIRECTOR toggle unlocks the deeper tools — camera moves, props, and scenes —
for when you want to art-direct the video instead of just playing. Start simple in
PLAY mode first, then flip on DIRECTOR when you want more control.

## Tips
- Start in PLAY to get a feel for the level your song built.
- Use REC to capture a clean run.
- Turn on DIRECTOR only when you want camera/prop/scene control.

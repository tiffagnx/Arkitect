Treatment — how it works
  1. Vibe the idea with Tiff, drop the song (she hears its length + BPM) and reference images.
  2. Build your cast & locations with her — they lock into slots and become the reference for every shot, so faces & places stay identical.
  3. Hit Write treatment from convo — she formats each shot with CAST:/LOC: tags + [IMAGE] and [VIDEO] blocks (and [m:ss-m:ss] timecodes for music videos).
  4. Break Down generates every still, each locked to its cast/location refs. Review them — regenerate, re-prompt, or pull one from your gallery.
  5. Generate all videos animates each still (5s or 10s, auto), then Open in LePrince drops the whole cut on a timeline, synced to the song.

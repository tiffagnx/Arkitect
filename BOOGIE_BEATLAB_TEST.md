# Yo Boogie — break this for us 🥁

This is a beat maker (**DeMartin Beat Lab**) that lives inside our recording program (**DeMartin Audio Labs**). You're the only one who can really tell us if it feels right — none of us make beats like you. Don't be nice. If something feels off, that's the whole point.

**One thing to know:** there's a gold **📣 Feedback** button in the bottom-right of every room. Anytime something sucks, is missing, or you wish it did *X* — hit it, talk or type it, send. That's how it gets back to us. Use it a lot.

---

## Part 1 — Just make a beat (Beat Lab on its own)

Open **Beat Lab** and mess with it like you would FL or whatever you use.

1. **Does it bang out of the box?** Hit play — there's a beat already loaded. Good starting feel or no?
2. **The drums + 808s** — punchy enough? Tuned right? 808 glides/slides feel real?
3. **The instruments** — are there enough? Pianos, keys, guitars, pads, the stuff you actually reach for? What's missing that you'd need on day one?
4. **The piano roll** — does writing a melody / chord progression feel natural, or fighting you?
5. **The AI in the plugins** — every plugin has a 🧠 you can *talk to* ("make it warmer," "more punch," "less boomy") and it turns its own knobs. Does it actually do what you ask? Does it sound better after?
6. **Make one beat you'd actually use.** Save it (File ▸ Save). Did anything get lost or sound different when it came back?

## Part 2 — The new thing: send a beat into the studio

This is the part we just built. The idea: cook a beat, then drop the whole thing onto a track in the real studio so it can get mixed with vocals.

7. In **Audio Labs** (the studio), top bar, hit **🥁 BEAT LAB**. The whole Beat Lab should pull up in a window *inside* the studio.
8. Make a beat in there (or it'll have one ready).
9. Hit **⤓ Send to Studio** (top-right of that window).
10. **A track should drop onto the timeline.** → **Does it sound EXACTLY like what you made?** Same drums, same 808, same levels, nothing missing or weird? This is the #1 thing we need you to check.
11. Try it a few times — does each beat land clean?

## Part 3 — Tell us what it SHOULD be

12. If this was *your* tool — what would make you actually use it instead of what you've got? Dream big, we'll build it.
